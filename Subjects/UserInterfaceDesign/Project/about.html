About us

Welcome to the Texas Roadhouse, a place where you can enjoy authentic Texas flavors right in your own city. Here you will find an unrivaled combination of quality food, cozy atmosphere and friendly service.

Our dishes are prepared using fresh ingredients and secret recipes passed down from generation to generation. Our team of experienced chefs pays special attention to every detail to ensure that every bite of our fast food is a true work of art.

We specialize in juicy steaks cooked to your liking - from rare to well done. But that is not all! Our menu also includes mouth-watering ribs, juicy burgers, fresh salads and more. We have something for every foodie.

At Texas Roadhouse, we value Southern hospitality. Our team of waiters will provide you with attentive service and a smile from the first moment you walk through the door. We strive to make your visit unforgettable and enjoyable.

The Texas Roadhouse Restaurant is a place to enjoy authentic Texas fast food in a friendly and welcoming environment. Come visit us and discover the taste of the South right in your own city. We are confident that you will be satisfied and want to come back again and again.

In addition to excellent cuisine and a cozy atmosphere, the Texas Roadhouse restaurant also offers its visitors a variety of entertainment events. We host theme nights with live country music, where you can immerse yourself in the atmosphere of southern rock and enjoy dancing to the sound of a guitar.

Every week we hold competitions and sweepstakes to delight our visitors with pleasant gifts and surprises. You can win special coupons for free appetizers or discounts on your next visit to our restaurant.

We also organize master classes on cooking Texas dishes, where our chefs will share cooking secrets and techniques. You can learn how to make authentic Texas steaks or make famous sauces and marinades to impress your friends and family at home.
